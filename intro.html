Detection of defects including cracks and flakes on wall surface in high-rise buildings is a crucial task of buildings’ maintenance. If left undetected and untreated, these defects can signiﬁcantly aﬀect the structural integrity and the aesthetic aspect of buildings, timely and cost-eﬀective methods of building condition survey are of practicing need for the building owners and maintenance agencies to replace the time- and labour-consuming approach of manual survey. The objective of the project is to build a web application to detect the type of building defect . The input is taken from the in built web cam, which in turn is given to the pre trained model . The model predicts the type of building defect and displayed on OpenCV window.
